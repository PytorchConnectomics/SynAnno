SynAnno is a tool designed for proofreading and correcting synaptic polarity annotation from electron microscopy (EM) volumes - specifically the H01 dataset. On this landing page you can choose between two workflows: "Revise Dataset" and "Proofread Annotation". The former allows you to redraw segmentation masks, assign pre-/post-synaptic IDs, and add missed false negatives. The latter allows you to review existing segmentations and pre-/post-synaptic IDs, mark incorrect instances, and assign error descriptions to those instances. After categorizing all erroneous instances in the "Proofread Annotation" workflow, you can automatically proceed with the "Revise Dataset" workflow. Each page has three buttons: "Home", "Question Mark", and "Menu". The first returns you to the landing page. The second provides an explanation of the current view and its functionality. The third provides general information and contact details.
