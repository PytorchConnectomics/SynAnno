SynAnno is a tool for proofreading and correcting synaptic polarity annotations from electron microscopy (EM) volumes, specifically the H01 dataset. On this landing page, you can choose between two workflows: "Revise Dataset" and "Proofread Annotation."

"Revise Dataset" allows you to redraw segmentation masks, assign pre-/post-synaptic IDs, and add missed false negatives. "Proofread Annotation" lets you review existing segmentations, verify pre-/post-synaptic IDs, mark incorrect instances, and assign error descriptions. After categorizing all erroneous instances in "Proofread Annotation," you can automatically proceed to "Revise Dataset."

Each page has a navigation bar at the top with three icons:

  • Returns to the landing page
  • Explain the current view and its functionality
  • Provides general information and contact details
